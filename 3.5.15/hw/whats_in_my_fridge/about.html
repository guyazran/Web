About My Fridge

General

This is an Amona refrigerator purchased in 2007. It is 1.7 meters tall and 1.1 meters wide. The chrome finish is not magnetic, hence there aren't any magnets on it.

Features

There are two doors on the top part of the fridge and a bottom tray tha acts as a freezer. the fridge is set to a default temperature of 6 degrees Celsius. There are compartments on the door for extra storage options.
